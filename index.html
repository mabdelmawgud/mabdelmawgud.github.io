Mubasher.info

A platform for equity market news, data, analysis and investor tools

Mubasher.info is the Middle East and North Africa’s most-preferred Arabic-language platform for equity market news, data, analysis and investor tools. More than 2.5 million visitors use the web portal each month to acquire decision-enabling information and content on 14 markets of the region.
Mubasher.info has offices in Saudi Arabia, Kuwait and the United Arab Emirates, with the operations hub based in Cairo, Egypt. It also has a network of journalists and information providers spread across more than 10 other countries.
Mubasher.info’s mission is to enable investors in the Middle East and North Africa gain valuable intelligence on equity and fixed-income markets, delivered whenever they need it, wherever they need it, in any format they want it, and on any platform of their choice. You can access Mubasher.info on the web, as well as through engaging IOS and Android apps.
